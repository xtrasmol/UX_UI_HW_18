Résumé

Nice to meet you! I'm

Christa Lee, a UX Designer based in Washington, DC.

I’m a doodler and user-centric problem-solver, with backgrounds in Industrial Design and Architecture.

I take immense enjoyment and satisfaction from noodling through multi-faceted problems, from user research to ideation to wireframing and prototyping. Empathizing with users to convey the story and adding personality that provides an emotional connection to elevate the user’s experience are my specialties. I consider these to be some of the most important aspects of design, and they drive me to bridge the gap between stakeholders, developers, and users.

In my spare time, I love bouldering, playing tabletop games, baking sweets, and yelling at my cats.
